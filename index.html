Who can apply

Are you the
right person?

We give special consideration to passionate individuals without university degrees who demonstrate exceptional aptitude and drive.
  • 🎓
    Recent graduates or near-graduates

    Preferably first-class or 2:1 holders, though exceptional candidates at any grade are considered.

  • 💻
    Demonstrable coding ability

    Python, C, or C++ — you don't need to be an expert but you need to have started. Show us your projects.

    Not a hard requirement — passion and aptitude matter more than your degree title.

  • 🌍
    Open globally

    Any nationality. You just need to be legally permitted to study in Nigeria and able to be physically present in Ibadan.

  • 💻
