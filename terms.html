1. Introduction

These Terms and Conditions ("Terms") govern the RAIN 2025 Scholarship Program offered by Robotics and Artificial Intelligence Nigeria (RAIN), a company incorporated under the laws of Nigeria with RC Number RC1607482.

Purpose of Scholarship

The RAIN Scholarship Program is designed to advance workforce development in emerging technologies of the Fourth Industrial Revolution (4IR) across Nigeria and West Africa, specifically in robotics, automation, artificial intelligence, and machine learning.

By applying for or accepting the RAIN Scholarship, you ("Applicant" or "Scholar") agree to be bound by these Terms and Conditions in their entirety.

2. Definitions

  • "RAIN" refers to Robotics and Artificial Intelligence Nigeria, RC Number RC1607482
  • "Scholarship" refers to the partial tuition coverage and benefits provided under this program
  • "Scholar" refers to a successful scholarship recipient
  • "RDA Program" refers to the Robot Development and Automation curriculum
  • "AIML Program" refers to the Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning curriculum
  • "Combined Program" refers to the integrated 24-month program covering both RDA and AIML
  • "Training Period" refers to the duration of the selected program (12 or 24 months)

3. Eligibility Requirements

3.1 Academic Qualifications

  • Recent graduates with degrees in Engineering, Computer Science, Computing, Mathematics, or Physical Sciences
  • Preferably First-class degree holders with excellent references from university lecturers
  • Special consideration may be given to passionate individuals without university degrees who demonstrate exceptional aptitude

3.2 Technical Requirements

  • Demonstrable knowledge of programming languages (Python, C++, or C preferred)
  • Genuine passion for robotics, automation, and/or artificial intelligence
  • Ability to provide own laptop meeting minimum specifications (16GB RAM, Core i7 recommended)

3.3 Legal Requirements

  • Must be legally permitted to study in Nigeria
  • Open to qualified candidates globally
  • Must be able to relocate to Ibadan, Nigeria for the training period

Important Note

Providing false information in your application will result in immediate disqualification and potential legal action.

7. Termination of Scholarship

7.1 RAIN's Right to Withdraw or Terminate

Scholarship Withdrawal

RAIN reserves the absolute right to withdraw the scholarship offer at any time and terminate studentship for any of the following reasons:

  • Academic underperformance or failure to meet program requirements
  • Attendance below 90% without valid justification
  • Any form of indiscipline or misconduct
  • Lack of commitment or dedication to the program
  • Violation of RAIN's code of conduct
  • Provision of false information in application
  • Failure to comply with these Terms and Conditions
  • Failure to pay tuition fee obligations in due time
  • Acting on behalf of RAIN without authorization

7.2 Voluntary Withdrawal

  • Scholars may withdraw voluntarily with 30 days written notice
  • No refund of utilized scholarship benefits
  • Return of any RAIN property including starter kit materials

7.3 Consequences of Termination

  • Immediate cessation of all scholarship benefits
  • No certification or completion recognition
  • Potential liability for scholarship amounts utilized (in cases of misconduct)
  • Return of all RAIN materials and resources
