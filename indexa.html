Who can apply

Are you the
right person?

We give special consideration to passionate individuals without university degrees who demonstrate exceptional aptitude and drive.
  • 🎓
    Recent Uni, Poly or High School graduates or near-graduates

    Preferably but not limited to first-class or 2:1 holders, though exceptional candidates at any grade are considered.

  • 💻
    Demonstrable coding ability

    Python, C, or C++ — you don't need to be an expert but you need to have started. Show us your projects via GitHub or portfolio link.

  • 🔬
    Engineering, Computing, Maths or Sciences background

    Not a hard requirement — passion and aptitude matter more than your degree title.

  • 🌍
    Open globally

    Any nationality. You just need to be legally permitted to study in Nigeria and physically present in Ibadan.

  • 💻
    Your own laptop

    16GB RAM, Core i7 recommended. You also arrange your own accommodation — RAIN can help facilitate options in Ibadan.

Before you apply — be clear on this

  • The scholarship covers tuition and starter kit only
  • No monthly stipend is provided
  • No accommodation is provided (RAIN can help you find options)
  • No laptop is provided — bring your own (16GB RAM, Core i7 recommended)
  • Training is highly hands-on with individual and group projects
  • You must be physically present in Ibadan for the duration
